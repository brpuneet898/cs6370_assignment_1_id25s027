PART 4 - QUESTION 1
======================================================================

Task performed:
Stopwords were removed from the tokenized Cranfield documents using a curated stopword list.
The curated list used here is the NLTK English stopword list.

Pipeline used:
1. Sentence segmentation: NLTK Punkt
2. Word tokenization: NLTK Penn Treebank tokenizer
3. Stopword removal: NLTK curated English stopword list

Corpus statistics:
Number of documents: 1400
Total sentences: 9969
Total tokens before stopword removal: 239132
Total tokens after stopword removal: 146114
Total stopwords removed: 93018

Interpretation:
Common function words such as articles, prepositions, conjunctions, and auxiliary verbs are removed.
This reduces noise in the document representation and keeps more content-bearing words for later IR tasks.
